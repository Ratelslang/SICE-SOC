🇿🇦 SA OPERATIONAL INTEL — GAUTENG
⚠ Static reference intel — always verify with SAPS bulletins, community WhatsApp groups and live Waze/Google Maps before deployment.
JOHANNESBURG CBD & INNER CITY
HIGH RISK
  • Avoid M1/N1 on-ramps at Bree/Sauer after 19:00 — known smash-and-grab corridors
  • Noord St taxi rank area — high pedestrian conflict, route around during peak hours
  • Hillbrow / Berea / Yeoville: advance work only; never casual routing through
  • Park Station surrounds: SAPS patrols inconsistent; avoid stationary vehicle stops
  • Fordsburg / Crown Mines late night: reduce speed, check mirrors at red lights
SANDTON / ROSEBANK / MIDRAND
MEDIUM RISK
  • Sandton Drive / William Nicol: heavy congestion 07:00–09:00 and 16:30–18:30; buffer 2× ETE
  • Nicol Hwy / Rivonia Rd interchange: common following pattern start point — vary exit routes
  • Rosebank Mall: pedestrian overload weekends; vehicle access can close during events
  • Midrand N1 southbound: construction zones shift monthly — verify lane configs pre-advance
  • Woodmead: OBO (opportunistic break-and-enter) spike at retail parking
PRETORIA / TSHWANE
MEDIUM RISK
  • Church St / Paul Kruger: govt areas have unannounced closures during official movements
  • Sunnyside / Arcadia: protest activity near Union Buildings access routes
  • Menlyn: high vehicle density weekends; alternate extraction via Garsfontein Rd
  • N14 Centurion: E-Tag + cash for toll plazas; backup lanes open
  • Hatfield: student density; random protest marches — monitor media morning of job
EAST RAND / EKURHULENI
HIGH RISK
  • N12 / N17 east of CBD: hijacking hotspot after dark — convoy only, no solo stops
  • OR Tambo Airport: follow-home incidents from international arrivals — counter-surv from pick-up point
  • Germiston / Katlehong surrounds: keep vehicle moving; no unplanned stops
  • Boksburg N12: avoid red-light stops where possible
SOWETO / WEST RAND
HIGH RISK
  • Soweto Highway (D30): community blockades common during municipal disputes
  • Dobsonville / Protea Glen: unlit roads; avoid after 20:00 without local escort
  • Krugersdorp N14: mining belt — increased organised criminal gang presence
  • Roodepoort / Constantia Kloof: OBO/robbery trend at mall parking areas
GAUTENG HIGHWAY & TOLL NOTES
REFERENCE
  • E-TOLL system contested — keep R50 cash per plaza regardless
  • N1 North (Bela-Bela direction): no cell signal after Boekenhoutskloof — comms plan needed
  • N3 Durban corridor: high-value cargo hijackings — convoy plan if using
  • N14 to NW Province: avoid night driving; limited emergency services
  • SANRAL emergency: 0800 726 725 (24hr)
  • AA roadside: 086 100 0234 — save local tow contact per operational area
